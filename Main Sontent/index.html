Are you new to Townsville?

General information about Townsville is available at - Townsville City Council Website

Townsville has a population of about 200,000 and is growing at about 1 suburb per year, so there is a lot of musical activity. All private schools and most government schools have music teachers. The larger private schools are Townsville Grammar, the Cathedral School and Ryan Catholic College. The larger public high schools are Kirwan and Pimlico. Music Teachers Association of Queensland is a good source of information for local music teachers.

The Townsville Branch contact is the Secretary (Ms Margery Jorgensen) Phone (07) 47790382 ; Email: mjo11750@bigpond.net.au.
